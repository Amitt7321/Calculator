Tribute to A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931-2015) was India's 11th President, celebrated as the "Missile Man of India" and the "People's President." Rising from a humble background in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he became a pioneering aerospace scientist who revolutionized the nation's space and defense programs.

Achievments

  • Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was a visionary scientist and India's 11th President (2002-2007).
  • Often called the "Missile Man of India," he is celebrated for pioneering indigenous satellite launch vehicles, spearheading the Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme.
